    Meet and Greet - Sept. 25th

    On September 25th, CS Club had our first Meet and Greet of the year. We had a good time chatting, getting to know each other, and enjoying delicious Sushi from Ichiban. Thank you Ressi and Humaira for putting this together. Check out some cool photos we took in our Gallery!

    Google Hangouts with a Googler

    CS Club held an event where a Googler came to talk to us through Google Hangouts about the various opportunities at Google for students in technology. In an hour, CS club members had a better idea of what it's like to work in one of the best tech companies in the world. Snack and drink were provided together with raffles for some cool swags from Google. Thank you Wendy for helping us out.

    Y-Hack 2013 at Yale

    On November 8th, CS Club members will travel together to attend one of the biggest regional Hackathons of the year at Yale University. The event will be happening in 24 hours. This year MHC CS Club sends more than 30 students to participate. Wait for more awesome pictures and stories from our members after the event. Good luck everyone!
